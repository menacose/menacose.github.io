We are pleased to invite you to our upcoming **Expert Talk** delivered by Dr. Maged Elaasar, President of INCOSE MENA, on the topic:

### "Sub-Chapter Framework Working Group (SCFWG)"

This session will introduce the vision, objectives, and proposed structure of the MENACOSE Sub-Chaptering Framework, highlighting how it will enable sustainable local activities, strengthen regional collaboration, and ensure alignment with INCOSE principles and MENACOSE’s strategic direction.

#### Event Details:
- **Speaker:** Dr. Maged Elaasar
- **Date:** Sunday, 15.02
- **Time:** 07:00 PM (Cairo Time)
- **Venue:** Online via Zoom
- **Audience:** INCOSE MENA Members Only

We encourage all members interested in shaping the future of MENACOSE sub-chapters and regional engagement to attend this insightful session.

We look forward to your participation.
