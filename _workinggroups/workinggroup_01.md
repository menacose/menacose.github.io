<h1>Working Group 01: Sub-chaptering Framework</h1>
<p>The <strong>Working Group 01 (Sub-chaptering Framework)</strong> aims to define a clear structure and guidelines for establishing local chapters across the MENA region, ensuring coherence and alignment.</p>

<h2>Purpose</h2>
<p>The <strong>Sub-Chapter Framework Working Group (SCFWG)</strong> is established to design and recommend a standardized framework for creating and managing sub-chapters under MENACOSE in the MENA region. This framework will define objectives, governance, roles, and operational rules to ensure alignment with INCOSEâ€™s vision while respecting regional diversity.</p>

<h2>Objectives</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Define the mission and objectives of MENACOSE sub-chapters</li>
  <li>Establish governance and reporting structures between the main chapter and sub-chapters</li>
  <li>Develop criteria for forming, approving, and sustaining sub-chapters</li>
  <li>Provide guidance on leadership, elections, and member engagement</li>
  <li>Outline rules for financial management, events, and knowledge sharing</li>
  <li>Recommend inclusive and equitable practices across the region</li>
</ul>

<h2>Deliverables</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Draft Sub-Chapter Framework (v0.1) for feedback</li>
  <li>Final <strong>Sub-Chapter Framework Handbook (v1.0)</strong>, approved by the MENACOSE Board and INCOSE HQ</li>
  <li>Pilot implementation report capturing lessons learned</li>
  <li>Templates and guidelines for proposals, elections, reporting, and events</li>
</ul>

<h2>Membership &amp; Governance</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Open to all MENACOSE members in good standing</li>
  <li><strong>Dr. Maged Elaasar serves as WG Chair, supported by Dr. Bassem Hassan as Deputy Chair(s) and Hassan Eldeeb as Secretary</strong></li>
  <li>Operates under the authority of the MENACOSE Chapter Board</li>
  <li>Decisions made by consensus, or by majority vote when necessary</li>
  <li>Monthly meetings; quarterly progress reports submitted to the Board</li>
</ul>
