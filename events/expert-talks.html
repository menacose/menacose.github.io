The INCOSE MENA Expert Talk Series is a platform where thought leaders and industry experts share their knowledge, experiences, and insights on cutting-edge topics in systems engineering. These talks aim to inspire, educate, and foster collaboration within the systems engineering community.

Some of our notable Expert Talks include:

  • AI-Powered Systems Engineering by Dr. Bassem Hassan: Exploring the transformative role of artificial intelligence in advancing systems engineering practices.
  • AI Copilot for Systems Engineering by Kareem Elaasar: Demonstrating how AI tools can assist and enhance the work of systems engineers.
  • Introduction to Digital Twin by Mohamed Adel: Providing an overview of digital twin technology and its applications in modern engineering.
  • Introduction to Systems Engineering by Dr. Bassem Hassan: A foundational talk that delves into the principles and practices of systems engineering, widely regarded as a must-watch for newcomers to the field.

Join us to gain valuable insights and stay at the forefront of systems engineering advancements.
