How to Join MENACOSE

Step 1: Join INCOSE

To become a member of the INCOSE MENA Chapter, you must first join the International Council on Systems Engineering (INCOSE). INCOSE membership provides you with:

  • Access to systems engineering knowledge and resources
  • Networking opportunities with professionals worldwide
  • Professional development and certification programs
  • Participation in working groups and technical activities

Join INCOSE

Step 2: Select the MENA Chapter

During the INCOSE membership registration process, you will be able to choose your local chapter. Select the MENA Chapter to connect with systems engineering professionals in the Middle East and North Africa region.

You can also visit our chapter page directly:

Visit INCOSE MENA Chapter Page

Step 3: Get Involved

Once you've joined INCOSE and selected the MENA Chapter, you can:

  • Attend chapter meetings and events
  • Join working groups aligned with your interests
  • Participate in technical discussions and knowledge sharing
  • Contribute to chapter initiatives and projects
  • Network with fellow systems engineering professionals
Questions?

If you have any questions about joining MENACOSE, please contact us and we'll be happy to help!
