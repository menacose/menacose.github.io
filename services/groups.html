We are excited to announce the establishment of the Sub-Chapter Framework Working Group (SCFWG) as part of MENACOSE. This Working Group is tasked with designing a standardized framework for creating and managing MENACOSE sub-chapters across the region. The framework will ensure alignment with MENACOSE’s vision while facilitating in-person activities and interactions on the ground.

The SCFWG aims to provide a structured approach to sub-chapter development, enabling consistent and effective operations that support MENACOSE’s mission of advancing systems engineering in the region. By participating in this Working Group, you will have the opportunity to contribute to shaping the future of MENACOSE and strengthening our community across the MENA region.

For full details about the SCFWG, please visit our website: SCFWG Details. Note that our website is still under construction, and some sections may not yet display relevant or correct information.

We invite and encourage you to join this Working Group. Kindly register your interest using the following form: SCFWG Registration Form. Your participation will play a vital role in shaping the future of MENACOSE and fostering a stronger systems engineering community in the region.
