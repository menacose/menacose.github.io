INCOSE MENA provides a platform for professionals and academics to publish their work and contribute to the advancement of systems engineering. By participating in INCOSE conferences such as the International Symposium (IS) and International Workshop (IW), you can showcase your research, share insights, and engage with a global audience of systems engineering experts.

These conferences offer opportunities to present papers, participate in panel discussions, and collaborate on cutting-edge topics that shape the future of systems engineering. Whether you are exploring innovative methodologies, addressing regional challenges, or sharing case studies, INCOSE conferences provide a prestigious venue to amplify your work.

In addition to conference participation, INCOSE MENA actively collaborates with professionals and institutions to publish academic work focused on the state of systems engineering practices in the MENA region. This includes conducting surveys, publishing reports, and contributing to thought leadership that highlights the unique challenges and opportunities within the region.

By engaging with INCOSE MENA, you can contribute to a growing body of knowledge that informs best practices, drives innovation, and strengthens the systems engineering community. Join us in shaping the future of systems engineering through impactful publications and collaborations.
